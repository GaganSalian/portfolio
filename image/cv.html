Carried Away life 🙈

"I’ve always been inspired by hardworking people who solve real problems and make life easier for others. That’s how millionaires and billionaires are created — not overnight, but by starting small, earning people’s trust, and scaling up step by step. So I asked myself: ‘What problem can I solve in my own area?’ I come from a fishing community where most of the local economy depends on fishing. Here, people still rely on huge ledger books to record accounts. The books are heavy, so they carry a small notebook to the sea, then rewrite everything into the big ledger at home. It’s time-consuming, risky, and inefficient. That’s when the idea for my startup project – LedgerBook was born. It’s a digital version of their account book: simple, portable, and powerful. My vision goes beyond just building an app — I want to create a business. I’ll start small, adding ads and fishing-related marketplaces inside the app. If it succeeds locally, I’ll expand across my country, supporting multiple languages to empower more communities. This is not just a project. It’s my first step toward thinking and acting like a founder — solving real-world problems with technology, and slowly building my empire.”
